Privacy
Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and personalize your experience. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app's core functionality.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use essential infrastructure services (such as hosting providers) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements. These providers are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing any privacy-related issues promptly and transparently.
